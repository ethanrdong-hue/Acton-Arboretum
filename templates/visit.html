What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Water bottle
  • Camera for photos
  • Bug spray (especially in summer)
  • Field guide or phone app for tree identification

Visitor Guidelines

  • Stay on designated paths
  • Do not pick flowers or damage plants
  • Pets welcome on leash
  • Photography allowed (non-commercial)
  • Picnicking permitted in designated areas
  • No bicycles on trails
