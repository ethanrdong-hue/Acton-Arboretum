About Us

The Acton Arboretum is dedicated to the cultivation, study, and appreciation of trees and woody plants. With over 500 species of trees and plants, we provide visitors with an educational and peaceful experience surrounded by nature.

Whether you're a botanist, nature enthusiast, or just looking for a beautiful place to spend time, the Acton Arboretum welcomes you.
